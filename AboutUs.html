About Us

The company was founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini, an Italian industrialist and entrepreneur, in 1963. Ferruccio Lamborghini initially started his career as a tractor manufacturer. He established a successful business called Lamborghini Trattori, which produced agricultural machinery.
